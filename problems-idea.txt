
Problem	IT Solution	Example Application
1. Lost-and-found chaos in schools/offices	RFID-based item tracking system	Students tag belongings; system alerts when items are misplaced

2. Difficulty in monitoring student attendance	Biometric or QR-based attendance system	Fingerprint or QR scan at classroom entry

3. Farmers struggling with unpredictable weather	IoT-enabled smart farming system
	Sensors track soil moisture, weather forecasts, and suggest irrigation

4. Long queues in cafeterias	
Mobile pre-order and cashless payment app	
Students/employees order food ahead and pick up directly

5. Difficulty in managing barangay records	Cloud-based barangay management system	Residents’ records stored digitally for faster services

6. Confusion in hospital queueing	Digital queue management with SMS alerts	Patients get notified when it’s their turn

7. Difficulty in tracking borrowed library books	Smart library system with RFID tags	Auto-detect borrowed/returned books

8. Garbage collection inefficiency	GPS-enabled waste management system	Tracks garbage trucks and optimizes routes

9. Difficulty in monitoring student grades	Online grade tracking portal	Parents and students access grades anytime

10. Barangay disaster response delays	Mobile disaster alert and response app	Sends real-time evacuation alerts and tracks safe zones
